Welcome to WorkTracker

A completely private, local-first time management tool designed for professional freelance and studio workflows.

1. The Core Loop

  • Auto-Clock: While idle, the Clock-In Time syncs to your system clock. The moment you press Start, that time is locked in for the day.
  • Shift Targets: Adjust your Planned Working Hours directly on the main tracker. The system dynamically calculates and pushes back your "Projected End of Shift" time.
  • Shift Completion: When your Active Work hits your target hours, the app will trigger a system notification and an audio chime.

2. Action Buttons

  • Start Work / Take Break: Your primary toggle. It controls the main timer.
  • End Work: Finalizes your session and permanently saves the data to your History ledger.
  • Reset: A destructive action. Wipes the current timer back to zero without saving.

3. History & Exports

  • The Ledger: Click on any shift in the History tab to open it, view exact metrics, or type specific notes. Older months are auto-collapsed to save space.
  • PDF Export: Generates a stylized invoice document of your entire history with calculated monthly totals.
  • JSON Backup/Restore (in Settings): Download a raw database file of your history to transfer computers or safely clear your cache.

4. Global Hotkeys

  • Spacebar Toggle between Start Work, Take Break, and Resume Work.
  • Esc Close popups or return to the Tracker tab.
