Welcome to WorkTracker

A completely private, local-first time management tool designed for professional freelance and studio workflows.

1. The Core Loop

  • Auto-Clock: While idle, the Clock-In Time perfectly syncs to your system clock. The moment you press Start, that time is locked in for the day.
  • Overtime Target: WorkTracker assumes a standard 8-hour shift. If you enter hours into the Planned OT box, it will dynamically calculate and push back your "Projected End of Shift" time.
  • Shift Completion: When your Active Work hits your target hours, the app will trigger a system notification and an audio chime. The projected end time will turn purple and alert you that you are working Unplanned OT.

2. Action Buttons

  • Start Work / Take Break: This is your primary toggle. It controls the main timer and logs your time into either the Active Work or Break Time categories.
  • End Work: Finalizes your current session, stops the timers, and permanently saves the data (Work, Break, and OT) to your Shift History ledger.
  • Reset: A destructive action. It completely wipes the current timer back to zero without saving anything to your history.

3. Manual Adjustments

If you forget to pause the timer when stepping away, or if you do offline work that wasn't tracked, click Adjust Time Manually at the bottom of the tracker. Entering time into the "Missed Break" box will subtract that time from your Active Work and add it to Break Time to balance the ledger.

4. History & Exports

  • The Ledger: The History tab safely stores your shifts, grouped automatically by month and year. Click on any shift to open it, view exact metrics, or type specific notes about what was accomplished that day.
  • PDF Export: Generates a stylized, printable invoice document of your entire history, automatically calculating your total hours and overtime for each month.
  • JSON Backup/Restore: Because this app is 100% private, your data lives strictly in your browser. Use these buttons to download a raw database file of your history so you can transfer it to a new computer or clear your browser cache safely.

5. Global Hotkeys

  • Spacebar Rapidly toggle between Start Work, Take Break, and Resume Work.
  • Esc Close popups, close this help manual, or instantly return to the Tracker tab from the History tab.
